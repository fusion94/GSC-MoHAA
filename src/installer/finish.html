Why support the Open Source community?

Software is not the only field affected by open source; many fields of study and social and political views have been affected by the growth of the concept of open source. Advocates in one field will often support the expansion of open source in other fields, including Linus Torvalds who is quoted as saying, "the future is open source everything." Often, open source is an expression where it simply means that a system is available to all who wish to work on it.
